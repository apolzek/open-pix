## Notes for using this template:

1. **Timing**: Schedule at least 2 weeks in advance
2. **SPI Capacity**: Bacen will coordinate sending 20k transactions to you simultaneously
3. **DICT Capacity**: Bacen pre-registers ~20k test keys before the test
4. **Re-tests**: If a phase fails, you can reschedule (usually within 1-2 weeks)
5. **Partner PSP**: For bilateral tests, coordinate with your partner PSP independently
